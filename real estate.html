Real Estate

Introduction

When you boil it down to the basics, real estate has a simple meaning. It's a piece of land and the property – such as a house, office building, apartment, strip center or warehouse – that sits on it. These structures can be both above and under the ground.

Types of Real Estate

  1. Residential real estate

    2. Any property used for residential purposes. Examples include single-family homes, condos, cooperatives, duplexes, townhouses, and multifamily residences.

  2. Commercial real estate

    3. Any property used exclusively for business purposes, such as apartment complexes, gas stations, grocery stores, hospitals, hotels, offices, parking facilities, restaurants, shopping centers, stores, and theaters.
